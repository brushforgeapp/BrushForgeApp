Privacy Policy

Effective: 2025-09-11

Overview

We collect the minimum necessary data to operate the app, process purchases, and improve performance.

Data We Collect

  • Account & Purchases. Apple receipts and identifiers for in‑app purchases.
  • Diagnostics. Crash logs and basic usage analytics.
  • Cloud Sync. If enabled, your paints/palettes/recipes can sync via iCloud/CloudKit.
  • AI Requests. Prompts are sent to our provider only to generate results. No reselling.

Third‑Party Services

  • Apple App Store / StoreKit
  • iCloud / CloudKit
  • Firebase (optional)
  • OpenAI or compatible provider
  • AdMob (free tier, if enabled)

Your Choices

  • Opt out of analytics in Settings
  • Local‑only mode (no sync)
  • Bring your own AI key
