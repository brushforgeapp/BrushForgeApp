Privacy Policy

Effective: 2025-09-23

Quick Overview

We collect the minimum necessary data to operate the app, process purchases, improve performance, and (with consent) show personalized ads.

Who We Are (Data Controller)

  • Controller: BrushForge
  • Contact: brushforgeapp@gmail.com
  • Scope: iOS app and this website
  • EEA/UK: We act as data controller for personal data processed via the app/site

Data We Collect

  • On-device Content: paints, palettes, recipes, preferences (stored locally; sync via iCloud/CloudKit if enabled)
  • Account & Purchases: Apple receipts/identifiers for in-app purchases (no card details)
  • Diagnostics: crash logs & basic usage metrics (aggregate; app version, device type, error codes)
  • Advertising IDs (consent): used by Google AdMob for personalized ads when permitted
  • AI Requests (optional use): prompts/content sent to our AI provider to generate results; no reselling

Legal Bases (GDPR)

  • Contract / Legitimate Interests: provide core features, maintain security, measure performance
  • Consent: personalized ads/tracking and any optional analytics beyond device settings
  • Legal Obligation: compliance, fraud prevention, and enforcement of terms

Advertising & Tracking (AdMob, ATT)

  • Provider: Google AdMob
  • ATT Prompt: we request permission where required; deny to disable tracking
  • Personalized Ads (with consent): uses device identifiers/inferred interests
  • Non-Personalized Ads: contextual ads shown without tracking identifiers
  • Controls: iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking; device ad settings

Third-Party Services

  • Apple App Store / StoreKit (purchases & receipts)
  • iCloud / CloudKit (optional sync)
  • Firebase (optional diagnostics/perf per OS settings)
  • OpenAI or compatible provider (AI features)
  • Google AdMob (ads; personalized only with consent)

We limit sharing to what’s necessary, use appropriate safeguards, and require providers to protect your data.

Data Retention

  • Diagnostics: up to 90 days (longer only to investigate issues), then deleted or anonymized
  • Account-linked Data (if any): while active and up to 24 months after last activity, or until deletion request
  • Backups: rolling backups up to 30 days
  • On-device Content: remains until you delete it or uninstall the app

International Transfers

  • Some providers may process data outside your country
  • We rely on safeguards (e.g., EU Standard Contractual Clauses) where required
  • We implement technical/organizational measures to protect your data

Your Rights

  • Access, correction, deletion, restriction, portability (where applicable)
  • Object to certain processing (where applicable)
  • Requests: email brushforgeapp@gmail.com (target response: 30 days)
  • Local data: delete in-app or uninstall
  • Subscriptions: iOS Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions
  • Diagnostics: iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Analytics & Improvements
  • Tracking: iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking
  • You may lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority

Children’s Privacy

  • Not intended for children under 13 (or local digital consent age)
  • No knowing collection from children; contact us to remove any such data

Security

  • TLS encryption in transit
  • Access controls & least-privilege practices
  • Continuous improvements; no method is 100% secure

Website Cookies & Analytics

  • Strictly necessary cookies for core site functions
  • Optional analytics/ads cookies only with consent where required
  • Manage via browser settings or any cookie controls we provide

Changes to This Policy

  • We may update this policy; we’ll post the new effective date
  • Material changes may be highlighted in-app or on this page
