Privacy Policy

Effective: 2025-09-23

We value your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how BrushForge (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses, and protects information in our iOS app and on this website.

1) Who we are (Data Controller)

BrushForge — Contact: brushforgeapp@gmail.com. If you are in the EEA/UK, we are the data controller for personal data processed through the app and this site.

2) What data we collect & why

  • On-device data you save (paint lists, palettes, recipes, preferences) — used to provide core features. Stored locally on your device. If you enable sync, these items may be stored with your Apple account via iCloud/CloudKit.
  • Purchases & subscriptions — processed by Apple (StoreKit). We receive purchase receipts/identifiers to unlock premium features. We do not receive your card details.
  • Crash/usage diagnostics (aggregate, if enabled on your device) — used to improve stability and features. May include app version, device type, timestamps, and error codes.
  • Advertising identifiers (only with your consent) — Google AdMob may use device identifiers (e.g., IDFA) to show ads. See “Advertising & Tracking.”
  • AI requests (if you use AI features) — prompts/content you submit are sent to our AI provider solely to generate results for you. We do not resell your data.

Data we do not intentionally collect

We do not intentionally collect sensitive categories (e.g., health, precise location) nor do we sell personal data or share it with data brokers.

3) Legal bases (GDPR)

  • Contract / legitimate interests: to provide core functionality, maintain security, and measure performance.
  • Consent: for personalized ads/tracking and any optional analytics beyond device-level settings.
  • Legal obligation: to comply with law, prevent fraud, or enforce terms.

4) Advertising & Tracking (AdMob, ATT)

We use Google AdMob to display ads. Where required, we request permission via Apple’s AppTrackingTransparency (ATT) prompt. If you deny permission or select non-personalized ads, AdMob will serve contextual/non-personalized ads. You can change iOS tracking permissions in: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

  • Personalized ads (with consent): AdMob may use device identifiers and inferred interests to show relevant ads.
  • Non-personalized ads: shown without using tracking identifiers.
  • Opt-out: deny ATT permission and/or disable ad personalization in device settings.

5) Third-party services we use

  • Apple App Store / StoreKit – purchases and receipts.
  • iCloud / CloudKit – optional sync of your content if enabled.
  • Firebase (optional) – diagnostics/perf if enabled in OS settings.
  • OpenAI or compatible provider – to process your AI requests when you use AI features.
  • Google AdMob – ads (personalized only with consent).

Each provider processes data in accordance with their own policies. We contractually require appropriate safeguards and process only what’s needed to provide the service.

6) Data retention

  • Crash logs & diagnostics: retained up to 90 days unless a longer period is needed to investigate issues, then deleted or anonymized.
  • Account-linked data (if any): retained while your account remains active and up to 24 months after last activity, or until you request deletion.
  • Backups: rolling backups up to 30 days.
  • On-device content: remains on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app.

7) International transfers

Some providers (e.g., Google, OpenAI) may process data outside your country. Where required, we rely on safeguards such as the EU Standard Contractual Clauses and implement security measures to protect your data.

8) Your rights

Subject to local law, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, restrict, or port your personal data, and to object to certain processing.

  • Request deletion or access: Email brushforgeapp@gmail.com. We aim to respond within 30 days.
  • Local data: delete the app or use in-app reset (if available) to remove on-device content.
  • Subscriptions: manage via Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions.
  • Diagnostics: toggle in Settings → Privacy & Security → Analytics & Improvements.
  • Tracking: control in Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.
  • Ad personalization: adjust in device settings and within the app (if we offer an in-app toggle).

You may also lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority (EEA/UK).

9) Children’s privacy

The app is not intended for children under 13 (or the age of digital consent in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided personal data, contact us to remove it.

10) Security

We use industry-standard measures including TLS encryption in transit, access controls, and least-privilege practices. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure; we continuously improve our safeguards.

11) Website cookies & analytics

This site may use strictly necessary cookies for core functionality. If we use optional analytics or advertising cookies on the website, we will seek consent where required. You can adjust preferences in your browser or via any cookie controls we provide.

12) Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will post the new effective date above. Material changes may be highlighted in-app or on this page.

13) Contact

Questions or requests: brushforgeapp@gmail.com
