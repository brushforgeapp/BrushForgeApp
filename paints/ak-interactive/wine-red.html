Frequently asked questions

What is the Pro Acryl equivalent of Wine Red?
The closest Pro Acryl match for AK Interactive Wine Red is Burnt Red (008, Pro Acryl Standard) with a ΔE2000 distance of 1.8 — nearly identical.
What color is Wine Red?
Wine Red is a standard red miniature paint from AK Interactive (3rd Gen Acrylic), code AK11096. Its screen color is approximately #69140D with a matte finish.
