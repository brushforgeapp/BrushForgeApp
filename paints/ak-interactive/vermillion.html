Frequently asked questions

What is the Scale75 equivalent of Vermillion?
The closest Scale75 match for AK Interactive Vermillion is Antares Red (SC37, Scalecolor) with a ΔE2000 distance of 1.6 — nearly identical.
What color is Vermillion?
Vermillion is a standard red miniature paint from AK Interactive (3rd Gen Acrylic), code AK11090. Its screen color is approximately #C44C33 with a matte finish.
