Frequently asked questions

What is the Army Painter equivalent of Nato Green?
The closest Army Painter match for AK Interactive Nato Green is Hardened Carapace (WP1430, Warpaints) with a ΔE2000 distance of 1.7 (nearly identical).
What color is Nato Green?
Nato Green is a standard brown miniature paint from AK Interactive (AFV Series), code AK796. Its screen color is approximately #45473A with a matte finish.
