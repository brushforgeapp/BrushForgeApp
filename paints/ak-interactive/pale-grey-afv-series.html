Frequently asked questions

What is the Vallejo equivalent of Pale Grey?
The closest Vallejo match for AK Interactive Pale Grey is Light Green Blue (70.972, Model Color) with a ΔE2000 distance of 5.9 (noticeable difference).
What color is Pale Grey?
Pale Grey is a standard green miniature paint from AK Interactive (AFV Series), code AK4133. Its screen color is approximately #74857D with a matte finish.
