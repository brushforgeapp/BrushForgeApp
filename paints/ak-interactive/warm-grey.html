Frequently asked questions

What is the Army Painter equivalent of Warm Grey?
The closest Army Painter match for AK Interactive Warm Grey is Necrotic Flesh (WP1108, Warpaints) with a ΔE2000 distance of 0.5 (nearly identical).
What color is Warm Grey?
Warm Grey is a standard orange miniature paint from AK Interactive (3rd Gen Acrylic), code AK11009. Its screen color is approximately #B6A88B with a matte finish.
