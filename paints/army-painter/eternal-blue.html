Frequently asked questions

What is the Vallejo equivalent of Eternal Blue?
The closest Vallejo match for Army Painter Eternal Blue is Pastel Blue (70.901, Model Color) with a ΔE2000 distance of 4.9 — close match.
What color is Eternal Blue?
Eternal Blue is a standard blue miniature paint from Army Painter (D&D Nolzur's Marvelous Pigments). Its screen color is approximately #5A7F93 with a matte finish.
