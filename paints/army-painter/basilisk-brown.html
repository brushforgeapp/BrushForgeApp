What is the AK Interactive equivalent of Basilisk Brown?
The closest AK Interactive match for Army Painter Basilisk Brown is Medium Flesh Tone (AK11054, 3rd Gen Acrylic) with a ΔE2000 distance of 3.9 — close match.
What color is Basilisk Brown?
Basilisk Brown is a standard orange miniature paint from Army Painter (Warpaints), code WP1405. Its screen color is approximately #CF8100 with a matte finish.
