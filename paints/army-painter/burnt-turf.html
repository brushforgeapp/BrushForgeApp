What is the Pro Acryl equivalent of Burnt Turf?

What color is Burnt Turf?
Burnt Turf is a standard orange miniature paint from Army Painter (Warpaints Fanatic), code WP3083P. Its screen color is approximately #BE9859 with a matte finish.
