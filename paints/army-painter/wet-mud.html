Frequently asked questions

What is the AK Interactive equivalent of Wet Mud?
The closest AK Interactive match for Army Painter Wet Mud is Bordeaux Red (AK11094, 3rd Gen Acrylic) with a ΔE2000 distance of 1.6 — nearly identical.
What color is Wet Mud?
Wet Mud is a standard brown miniature paint from Army Painter (Warpaints), code WP1478. Its screen color is approximately #9E3E35 with a matte finish.
