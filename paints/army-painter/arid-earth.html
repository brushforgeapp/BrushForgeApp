What is the Pro Acryl equivalent of Arid Earth?
The closest Pro Acryl match for Army Painter Arid Earth is Flameon Bright Pale Yellow (S30, Pro Acryl Signature) with a ΔE2000 distance of 4.0 — close match.
What color is Arid Earth?
Arid Earth is a standard orange miniature paint from Army Painter (Warpaints), code WP1402. Its screen color is approximately #FDEDC7 with a matte finish.
