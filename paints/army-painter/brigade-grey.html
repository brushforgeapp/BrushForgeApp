Frequently asked questions

What is the Vallejo equivalent of Brigade Grey?
The closest Vallejo match for Army Painter Brigade Grey is Heavy Bluegrey (72.144, Game Color) with a ΔE2000 distance of 2.6 (close match).
What color is Brigade Grey?
Brigade Grey is a standard grey miniature paint from Army Painter (Warpaints Fanatic), code WP3006P. Its screen color is approximately #D0D0D2 with a matte finish.
