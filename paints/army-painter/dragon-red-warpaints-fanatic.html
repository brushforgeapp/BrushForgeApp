Frequently asked questions

What is the Vallejo equivalent of Dragon Red?
The closest Vallejo match for Army Painter Dragon Red is Fire Flame (74.024, Nocturna Models) with a ΔE2000 distance of 1.4 (nearly identical).
What color is Dragon Red?
Dragon Red is a standard red miniature paint from Army Painter (Warpaints Fanatic), code WP3117P. Its screen color is approximately #BF283B with a matte finish.
