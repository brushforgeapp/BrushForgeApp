Frequently asked questions

What is the Kimera Kolors equivalent of Deep Blue?
The closest Kimera Kolors match for Army Painter Deep Blue is Phthalo Blue (Green Shade) (PB15.4, Base Set) with a ΔE2000 distance of 2.9 — close match.
What color is Deep Blue?
Deep Blue is a standard blue miniature paint from Army Painter (Warpaints), code WP1116. Its screen color is approximately #0E4E76 with a matte finish.
