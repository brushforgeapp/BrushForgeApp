Frequently asked questions

What is the Citadel equivalent of Dark Green?
The closest Citadel match for Vallejo Dark Green is Vulkan Green (Layer) with a ΔE2000 distance of 1.9 — nearly identical.
What color is Dark Green?
Dark Green is a standard green miniature paint from Vallejo (Game Color), code 72.028. Its screen color is approximately #1F3727 with a matte finish.
