Frequently asked questions

What is the AK Interactive equivalent of Olive Grey?
The closest AK Interactive match for Vallejo Olive Grey is Green Uniform Base -48- (AK3114, Figures Series) with a ΔE2000 distance of 0.7 — nearly identical.
What color is Olive Grey?
Olive Grey is a standard brown miniature paint from Vallejo (Model Color), code 70.888. Its screen color is approximately #40422D with a matte finish.
