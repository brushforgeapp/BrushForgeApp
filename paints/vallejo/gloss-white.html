Frequently asked questions

What is the AK Interactive equivalent of Gloss White?
The closest AK Interactive match for Vallejo Gloss White is White -1- (AK3027, Figures Series) with a ΔE2000 distance of 1.3 — nearly identical.
What color is Gloss White?
Gloss White is a standard white miniature paint from Vallejo (Model Color), code 70.842. Its screen color is approximately #F5F3F4 with a gloss finish.
