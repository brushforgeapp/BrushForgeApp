What is the Scale75 equivalent of Scarlet?
The closest Scale75 match for Vallejo Scarlet is Antares Red (SC37, Scalecolor) with a ΔE2000 distance of 2.6 — close match.
What color is Scarlet?
Scarlet is a standard red miniature paint from Vallejo (Model Color), code 70.817. Its screen color is approximately #C23D2E with a matte finish.
