Frequently asked questions

What is the Pro Acryl equivalent of Neutral Grey?
The closest Pro Acryl match for Vallejo Neutral Grey is Neutral Grey (075, Pro Acryl Standard) with a ΔE2000 distance of 1.6 (nearly identical).
What color is Neutral Grey?
Neutral Grey is a standard grey miniature paint from Vallejo (Model Color), code 70.992. Its screen color is approximately #61615F with a matte finish.
