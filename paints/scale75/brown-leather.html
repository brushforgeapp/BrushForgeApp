Frequently asked questions

What is the AK Interactive equivalent of Brown Leather?
The closest AK Interactive match for Scale75 Brown Leather is Reddish Black (AK3016, Figures Series) with a ΔE2000 distance of 1.6 — nearly identical.
What color is Brown Leather?
Brown Leather is a standard brown miniature paint from Scale75 (Scalecolor), code SC31. Its screen color is approximately #412D28 with a matte finish.
