Frequently asked questions

What is the AK Interactive equivalent of Necro Grey?
The closest AK Interactive match for Scale75 Necro Grey is Oxford (AK11188, 3rd Gen Acrylic) with a ΔE2000 distance of 1.1 — nearly identical.
What color is Necro Grey?
Necro Grey is a standard blue miniature paint from Scale75 (Fantasy & Games), code SFG42. Its screen color is approximately #373C46 with a matte finish.
