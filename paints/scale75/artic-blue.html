Frequently asked questions

What is the Two Thin Coats equivalent of Artic Blue?
The closest Two Thin Coats match for Scale75 Artic Blue is Ion Blue (Wave 3) with a ΔE2000 distance of 2.6 (close match).
What color is Artic Blue?
Artic Blue is a standard blue miniature paint from Scale75 (Scalecolor), code SC05. Its screen color is approximately #B4DCE1 with a matte finish.
