Frequently asked questions

What is the Army Painter equivalent of Boreal Green?
The closest Army Painter match for Scale75 Boreal Green is Evergreen Fog (WP3061P, Warpaints Fanatic) with a ΔE2000 distance of 1.3 — nearly identical.
What color is Boreal Green?
Boreal Green is a standard blue miniature paint from Scale75 (Scalecolor), code SC42. Its screen color is approximately #37645F with a matte finish.
