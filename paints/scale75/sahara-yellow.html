Frequently asked questions

What is the AK Interactive equivalent of Sahara Yellow?
The closest AK Interactive match for Scale75 Sahara Yellow is British Sand Yellow (AK11362, 3rd Gen Acrylic) with a ΔE2000 distance of 2.2 (close match).
What color is Sahara Yellow?
Sahara Yellow is a standard orange miniature paint from Scale75 (Scalecolor), code SC11. Its screen color is approximately #DCA54B with a matte finish.
