Frequently asked questions

What is the Two Thin Coats equivalent of Birch?
The closest Two Thin Coats match for Scale75 Birch is Vampire Fang (Wave 1) with a ΔE2000 distance of 2.0 (nearly identical).
What color is Birch?
Birch is a standard orange miniature paint from Scale75 (Scalecolor), code SC25. Its screen color is approximately #CDB99B with a matte finish.
