Frequently asked questions

What is the AK Interactive equivalent of Eclipse Grey?
The closest AK Interactive match for Scale75 Eclipse Grey is Basalt Grey / Black Uniform (AK3003, Figures Series) with a ΔE2000 distance of 1.3 (nearly identical).
What color is Eclipse Grey?
Eclipse Grey is a standard grey miniature paint from Scale75 (Scalecolor), code SC16. Its screen color is approximately #414141 with a matte finish.
