What is the AK Interactive equivalent of Burnt Sienna?
The closest AK Interactive match for Pro Acryl Burnt Sienna is Medium Rust (AK11103, 3rd Gen Acrylic) with a ΔE2000 distance of 3.7 — close match.
What color is Burnt Sienna?
Burnt Sienna is a standard red miniature paint from Pro Acryl (Pro Acryl Standard), code 059. Its screen color is approximately #AD4E32 with a matte finish.
