What is the Vallejo equivalent of Dark Yellow Green?
The closest Vallejo match for Pro Acryl Dark Yellow Green is Goblin Green (28.027, Hobby Paint) with a ΔE2000 distance of 1.0 — nearly identical.
What color is Dark Yellow Green?
Dark Yellow Green is a standard green miniature paint from Pro Acryl (Pro Acryl Signature), code S06. Its screen color is approximately #6A7546 with a matte finish.
