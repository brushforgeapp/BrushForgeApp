Frequently asked questions

What is the AK Interactive equivalent of White Blue?
The closest AK Interactive match for Pro Acryl White Blue is Rlm 76 Version 2 (AK11828, 3rd Gen Acrylic) with a ΔE2000 distance of 1.6 — nearly identical.
What color is White Blue?
White Blue is a standard grey miniature paint from Pro Acryl (Pro Acryl Signature), code S04. Its screen color is approximately #AFBDBA with a matte finish.
