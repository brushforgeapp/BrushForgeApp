What is the Citadel equivalent of Ivory?
The closest Citadel match for Pro Acryl Ivory is Wraithbone (Base) with a ΔE2000 distance of 2.2 (close match).
What color is Ivory?
Ivory is a standard yellow miniature paint from Pro Acryl (Pro Acryl Standard), code 023. Its screen color is approximately #D5D0B3 with a matte finish.
