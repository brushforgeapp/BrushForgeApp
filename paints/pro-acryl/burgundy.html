Frequently asked questions

What is the Vallejo equivalent of Burgundy?
The closest Vallejo match for Pro Acryl Burgundy is Gory Red (28.029, Hobby Paint) with a ΔE2000 distance of 1.7 — nearly identical.
What color is Burgundy?
Burgundy is a standard brown miniature paint from Pro Acryl (Pro Acryl Standard), code 069. Its screen color is approximately #733739 with a matte finish.
