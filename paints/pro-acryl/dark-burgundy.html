Frequently asked questions

What is the AK Interactive equivalent of Dark Burgundy?
The closest AK Interactive match for Pro Acryl Dark Burgundy is Reddish Black (AK11406, 3rd Gen Acrylic) with a ΔE2000 distance of 3.9 — close match.
What color is Dark Burgundy?
Dark Burgundy is a standard brown miniature paint from Pro Acryl (Pro Acryl Signature), code S15. Its screen color is approximately #2E1B1B with a matte finish.
