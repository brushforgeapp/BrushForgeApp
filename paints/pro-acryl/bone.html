Frequently asked questions

What is the Army Painter equivalent of Bone?
The closest Army Painter match for Pro Acryl Bone is Castle Grey (WP1407, Warpaints) with a ΔE2000 distance of 2.4 — close match.
What color is Bone?
Bone is a standard grey miniature paint from Pro Acryl (Pro Acryl Signature), code S23. Its screen color is approximately #8D857E with a matte finish.
