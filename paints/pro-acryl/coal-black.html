Frequently asked questions

What is the Scale75 equivalent of Coal Black?
The closest Scale75 match for Pro Acryl Coal Black is Decay Black (SFG00, Fantasy & Games) with a ΔE2000 distance of 1.2 — nearly identical.
What color is Coal Black?
Coal Black is a standard grey miniature paint from Pro Acryl (Pro Acryl Standard), code 002. Its screen color is approximately #18191B with a matte finish.
