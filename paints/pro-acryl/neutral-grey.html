Frequently asked questions

What is the Vallejo equivalent of Neutral Grey?
The closest Vallejo match for Pro Acryl Neutral Grey is Neutral Grey (70.992, Model Color) with a ΔE2000 distance of 1.6 — nearly identical.
What color is Neutral Grey?
Neutral Grey is a standard grey miniature paint from Pro Acryl (Pro Acryl Standard), code 075. Its screen color is approximately #5E5E5E with a matte finish.
