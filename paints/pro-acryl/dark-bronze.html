Frequently asked questions

What is the Vallejo equivalent of Dark Bronze?
The closest Vallejo match for Pro Acryl Dark Bronze is Olive Brown (70.889, Model Color) with a ΔE2000 distance of 3.6 — close match.
What color is Dark Bronze?
Dark Bronze is a standard brown miniature paint from Pro Acryl (Pro Acryl Signature), code S24. Its screen color is approximately #3F311F with a matte finish.
