Frequently asked questions

What is the Citadel equivalent of Khaki?
The closest Citadel match for Pro Acryl Khaki is Ushabti Bone (Layer) with a ΔE2000 distance of 1.9 — nearly identical.
What color is Khaki?
Khaki is a standard yellow miniature paint from Pro Acryl (Pro Acryl Standard), code 061. Its screen color is approximately #AC9E6B with a matte finish.
