Frequently asked questions

What is the Vallejo equivalent of Dark Camo Green?
The closest Vallejo match for Pro Acryl Dark Camo Green is Bronze Green (70.897, Model Color) with a ΔE2000 distance of 1.2 (nearly identical).
What color is Dark Camo Green?
Dark Camo Green is a standard green miniature paint from Pro Acryl (Pro Acryl Standard), code 036. Its screen color is approximately #313C2C with a matte finish.
