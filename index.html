Paint Converter

Accurate brand‑to‑brand matches with confidence scores and ΔE values. Understand where hue, lightness, and chroma differ — so you can compensate on the model.

  • ✅ Confidence scoring and ΔE LAB distance
  • ✅ Finish & type heuristics (e.g., Layer → Base)
  • ✅ AI suggestions and mix ideas (optional)

My Paints

Track owned and wishlist paints. Filter by brand, color family, type, or finish — everything stays fast and tidy.

  • ✅ Quick add / remove with clear status
  • ✅ Filter chips that match the app UI
  • ✅ Local-first with optional cloud sync

AI Recipes & Palettes

Generate step‑by‑step recipes and color‑theory palettes, then map them to real paints you own.

  • ✅ Guided (no free‑text prompts) to keep results on‑brand
  • ✅ “Closest paint matches” across brands
  • ✅ Save, export, and share

How it works

  1. ① Pick a source paint — we parse its color and metadata.
  2. ② We search across brands using ΔE + heuristics (type, finish, hue bins).
  3. ③ You get matches with clear differences (lightness, chroma, hue).
  4. ④ Save to My Paints, or build a palette/recipe.

Tip: Press Share in a recipe to export a clean image for your group.

FAQ

Is the converter accurate?

+
Matches use ΔE (color distance) plus rules for type/finish. You can thumbs‑up/down matches to improve them over time.

What do I get for free?

+
Full converter, 50 owned paints, and up to 5 saved palettes/recipes.

What does Premium unlock?

+
Removes limits/ads and unlocks AI helpers for recipes and palettes.
