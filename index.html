FAQ

Which paint brands are supported?

Citadel, Vallejo, Army Painter, AK, AK Real Colors, Duncan Rhodes, Green Stuff World, Kimera Kolors, and Privateer Press (P3).

Do AI features require internet?

Core features work offline; AI calls require connectivity and use a small credit system.

How accurate are matches?

Matches use LAB ΔE distance and show confidence %. Filter by brand, finish, or owned paints.

Is BrushForge affiliated with Games Workshop?

No — BrushForge is an independent tool for hobbyists and is not affiliated with Games Workshop.
