Frequently Asked Questions

?

Is the app really free?

Yes! The Hobbyist tier is free forever. It includes all core features: the paint converter, inventory tracking (limited to 40 paints), project management (1 project), and basic recipe tools. It is ad-supported.
?

Does my data sync between iOS and Android?

Yes, if you have a Premium subscription. You can log in with the same account on both platforms and your inventory, recipes, and projects will sync automatically via the cloud.
?

What paints are supported?

We support over 4,300 paints from major brands including Citadel, Vallejo (Model, Game, Air, Mecha), Army Painter (Warpaints, Fanatic, Speedpaint), AK Interactive (3rd Gen), Scale75, Pro Acryl, and many more.
?

What are AI Credits?

AI Credits are used for advanced features like the Recipe Generator and Scheme Analyzer. Free users get 5 credits per month, while Premium users get 100 credits per month.
