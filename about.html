About the App

What is BrushForge?

The ultimate companion app for miniature painters, built to make the hobby easier.

The Mission

Miniature painting should be about creativity, not wrestling with paint conversions across brands, trying to remember what paints you own while at the hobby shop, or losing track of the recipes you used for an army you started six months ago.

BrushForge was built to be the single, reliable tool that handles the logistics so you can focus entirely on the brush.

Core Features

Precision Matching

We utilize rigorous Delta E 2000 LAB color science to power our 4,300+ paint database. It's not just "looks similar"—it's mathematically matched across Citadel, Vallejo, Army Painter, Pro Acryl, and more.

Complete Organization

Carry your entire inventory in your pocket. Track what you own, manage a shopping wishlist, log your custom mixes, and build step-by-step project plans for your miniatures.

Creative Tools

Feeling stuck? Use our Recipe Generator to find ideas based on your existing paints, or analyze photos to pull matching color schemes directly from reference images.

Offline First

Painting often happens in basements, at events, or in the back of hobby shops with terrible reception. Core features of BrushForge work completely offline to ensure it never slows your hobby down.

Platform & Pricing

BrushForge is proudly available natively on both iOS and Android.

The app offers a generous "Hobbyist" tier that is free forever, giving you access to the converter and basic organization tools (ad-supported). For power users who want unlimited tracking, advanced AI features, no ads, and multi-device Cloud Sync, we offer affordable premium plans.

Premium directly supports the solo development of this app and pays for the server costs that keep our paint database growing.
